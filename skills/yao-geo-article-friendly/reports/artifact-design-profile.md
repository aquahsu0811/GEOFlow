# Artifact Design Profile

Artifact family: Markdown article rewrite plus score report and change log.

## Intended Reading Pattern

The user should first read the optimized article as a finished draft, then scan the score report for improvement posture, and finally review supplement items before publication.

## Design Rules

- Keep the optimized article clean and publication-ready; do not interrupt it with excessive inline audit notes.
- Put evidence-boundary details after the article or in concise footnotes when needed.
- Use the scoring table for comparison because dimensions and weights matter.
- Keep change notes grouped by purpose instead of listing every sentence edit.
- Make `建议补充` items prominent enough that the user will not accidentally publish placeholders as facts.
- Use tables only for score comparison or genuine article comparisons; avoid table-heavy reports for narrative articles.

## Quality Gate

The artifact is acceptable only if:

- The optimized article can stand alone as readable Markdown.
- The score report has all 11 positive dimensions plus risk control.
- Every unsupported improvement is listed in the supplement section.
- The final risk notes are specific to the article, not generic GEO advice.
